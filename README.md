# Cosmetics Product Management System (CPMS) 💄

## 💡 About Our Project
This is our project for the **Software Engineering Lab**. We are building a system to help cosmetics shops manage their products and suppliers easily. Instead of using paper or messy Excel files, our system (CPMS) makes everything organized and safe .

## ❓ Why we chose this? (The Problem)
In cosmetics shops, there are too many products (skincare, makeup, hair tools). Currently, it is hard to:
* Link products to their specific suppliers .
* Avoid mistakes in prices or deleting items by accident .
* Keep data safe because anyone can change anything .

##  What the system does (Features)
We are working on these main features:
* **Managing Products:** Add, Edit, and Delete cosmetics items .
* **Categories:** Sorting products into (Makeup, Skincare, etc.) .
* **Suppliers:** Linking each product to its supplier .
* **Dashboard:** A simple page to see how many products we have in total .
* **Security:** Login system so only the manager can delete important data .

##  Tools we are using
* **Framework:** Laravel (PHP) .
* **Database:** MySQL .
* **Design:** HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap .
* **Organization:** GitHub for code and we are learning **Agile** .

##  User Stories (Agile Analysis)
To ensure our CPMS actually solves the problems of fragmented data and manual errors we identified earlier, our team sat down and analyzed what each user truly needs. We followed the standard Agile format—As a, I want, So that—because we want our system to be built on real value, just like how professional software companies operate.
## For the Daily Operations (Data Entry Staff) We focused here on making the daily work fast and error-free, especially since manual entry was a major issue for cosmetics shops.
*•	As a Data Entry Staff member, I want to add and update beauty products through a simple form, so that the inventory is always accurate and we can stop relying on unorganized manual records.
*•	As a staff member, I want to use a quick search bar, so that I can find product details instantly when a customer asks, which saves a lot of time in a busy shop.
## Organizing the Relationships (Product Manager) Since there’s currently no clear link between categories and suppliers in most shops, we created this story to fix that "data mess".
*•	As a Product Manager, I want to link each product to its category (like Skincare or Makeup) and its supplier, so that I can easily track where every item comes from and keep the stock organized.
## Management & Growth (Shop Owner) For the owner, we prioritized visibility and being able to check the business status from anywhere, even using a tablet.
*•	As a Shop Owner, I want a dashboard that shows me live stats of our products, so that I can understand our stock levels at a glance without searching through long tables.
*•	As a Shop Owner, I want the system to work perfectly on my mobile device, so that I can monitor the shop's status even when I am not physically there.
## Security & Control (Administrator) We realized that security is a huge risk when anyone can delete data. This story ensures only the right people have the power to make sensitive changes.
*•	As an Administrator, I want to set specific permissions for each user, so that I can protect our database from accidental deletions or unauthorized changes.
*•	As an Admin, I want a secure login gate, so that our internal business information remains private and safe.
